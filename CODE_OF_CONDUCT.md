# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (v2)

As contributors and maintainers of Flow, we pledge to respect all people who
contribute through reporting issues, posting feature requests, updating
documentation, submitting pull requests, and other activities.

We are committed to a welcoming and inclusive community. Examples of behavior
that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Unacceptable behavior includes: harassment, discriminatory language,
insults, intimidation, or any other behavior that creates a hostile
environment.

Enforcement

If you are subject to or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the
project maintainers at the repository's issue tracker (mark the issue as private
if your platform supports it) or to the contact provided in repository settings.
Maintainers will review reports and may take action including warnings,
temporary or permanent bans, or other measures as appropriate.

For full details and examples, see the Contributor Covenant:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

Contact

If you need private assistance or to report a violation confidentially, contact
one of the repository owners listed in the project settings.
